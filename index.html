The studio

Maren Voss works slowly, in a converted print shop in Neukölln.

Trained at the Städelschule, she's spent the last decade returning to the same handful of subjects — rooms, harbors, the backs of chairs — letting the paint do the talking instead of the concept.

Her work has shown at Galerie Wagner, Kunsthalle Ost, and in private collections across Berlin, Lisbon, and Copenhagen. Commissions and studio visits by request.
